A Special Message For You

my dearest lulu,

to this day, it's officially been 836 days since we've met, and in every single one of those days, i've only grown happier, all thanks to you.

you've taught me how to look forward to another day, how to appreciate the little things in life, and most importantly, how to love truly, because i truly do love you.

from the moment we first met in that group chat, i didn't know what to expect. i barely knew you until people started tying us with bbangiz, and at first, i just went along with it as a joke because i didn't think we'd ever click. you were reserved and quiet (which i love about you), and i was just hooting and hollering 24/7. but from that first private message, i knew we were in for a long ride, and i'm so, so glad i decided to hop on that train.

you are always so selfless, putting people before yourself (which gives me mixed feelings, honestly), but you've taught me empathy because of it. sometimes, when i see something bad happen to someone, i'd normally just shrug it off, but then i imagine you beside me, whining about how poor they are, and i can't help but soften. i really, really love that about you, lulu. i love you, and i'll keep loving you even if the world tried to tear us apart.

every time i see a silly snoopy shirt that makes absolutely no sense, i think of you and how excited you'd be. i'd definitely buy it, half-annoyed but secretly thrilled, because i'd do anything just to see you smile. every time i see the color blue, tulips, or an ugly dog, or anything you love, i think of you and how happy it would make you. i'd do anything to grant you every dream you've ever had.

i want you to keep being you. i know it's cliché, but i mean it. never change just to please others. if they can't appreciate your loving heart and your wonderful soul, then they don't deserve even a single bit of you.

i don't know how much of this promise I can keep, but i'll try my hardest to leave this country and finally meet you. i want us to stay in a cozy cottage in the italian countryside, where i'll force you to eat strawberries with me every day. i'll bake you dozens of donuts until you're sick of them, but you'll keep eating just to please me. we'll visit that stupid pink wall in la and act like we've traveled back to 2016, wearing chokers and ugly round sunglasses.

we'll visit croata, and you'll show me every single place you grew up, every corner that holds a piece of your childhood. we'll find some dumb guinea pigs in a store and adopt one together. we'll cozy up at your house and watch brother bear in croatian dub so you can enjoy it to the fullest. that's all i want, for you to be happy and fulfilled. if i could, i'd sacrifice anything if it meant you'd get to be happy forever.

turning 22 might sound big, but always remember i'll be here to annoy you, to play 22 by taylor on repeat, and to remind you how loved you are. i'll always be here to encourage you through this year, and the next, and the next, because you can do anything you set your mind to. that's one of the most beautiful things about you, and i hope you never lose it.

lastly, happy 22nd birthday, my sweetest girl. enjoy this year to the fullest and cherish every single moment, because you only get to be 22 once. (and you better have 22 in your spotify wrapped, or you're getting blocked.)

happy birthday, my love.
i wish you all the happiness in the world.

love always,
miya
